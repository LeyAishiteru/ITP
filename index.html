TOP RATED ANIME

Attack on Titan

Rating: 9.0/10

In a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from Titans, gigantic humanoid creatures, Eren Yeager vows to eradicate the Titans after they bring about the destruction of his hometown and the death of his mother.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Rating: 9.1/10

Two brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric, search for the Philosopher's Stone to restore their bodies after a failed attempt to bring their mother back to life using alchemy.

Death Note

Rating: 8.9/10

Light Yagami finds a supernatural notebook that grants him the power to kill anyone whose name he writes in it. He decides to use it to rid the world of criminals, but his actions are challenged by a mysterious detective known only as L.
